Osmeña Peak

Osmeña Peak is the highest point in the island of Cebu, a province in the Philippines. Standing 1,013 meters above sea level, this stunning site offers a dramatic view of jagged cliffs with a view of the sea in the distance. Say hello to my friends at the back :)

Mantayupan Falls

Mantayupan Falls is a famous natural waterfalls resort located in Barili Municipality, Cebu Province, Philippines. Mantayupan Waterfall, one of the top tourist attractions in this municipality, is approximately 200 feet high from top to bottom water levels.
